Madison Area Crime Map

2017 Police Incident Reports

Reading this Map

This map displays crime locations between the years of 2013 and 2017. The goal of this map is to allow for users to explore crimes based on general area (specifically by Aldermanic Districts), incident type, if an arrest was made, and by time of occurrence (year, month, day, hour). Users may also explore surrounding cities and villages and compare crime statistics.

Aldermanic Districts

Definition: divided regions based on a municipal legislative body; especially a council.

Crime Index

Definition: crime index is a calculation that describes the number of crimes that are reported to a law enforcement agency per 100,000 people

Calculation: Number of reported crimes divided by the total population, which is then multiplied by 100,000
